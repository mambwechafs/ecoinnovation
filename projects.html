Environmental Solutions Portfolio: Our Range of Services and Expertise

At Eco-First Innovations, we take pride in our commitment to providing sustainable environmental solutions to our clients. Our portfolio showcases a range of successful projects and achievements that demonstrate our expertise in the field. We approach each project with a focus on innovation, environmental responsibility, and social awareness. Through our dedicated team of experts and industry-leading technologies, we have been able to help our clients achieve their environmental goals while also promoting sustainable practices. In this portfolio, we highlight some of our most successful projects and achievements, and we invite you to explore our solutions and capabilities.

Conducting EIA and writing EPB’s for Filling Stations Construction Harvest Group of Companies Limited

Between June 2020 and December 2021, Eco-First Innovations conducted a series of successful projects for the Harvest Group of Companies Limited in various locations across Lusaka and other districts of Zambia. Our team was tasked with conducting Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs) and writing Environmental Project Briefs (EPBs) for the construction of filling stations at these locations.

The project locations included Woodlands Site, Chalala-MTN-Tokyo Ring Road, Makeni Kavindele-New Ring Road, Middle West-New Ring Road, Lumumba Road (Industrial Area-Town), Lusaka West-Mungwi Road, Shimabala Site, Mwembeshi Site, Kasupe Site-New Ring Road, 13 Miles Site-Chibombo District, Nationalist Road-Libala South, Lilayi, New Kasama, Burma Road, Airport Road-Chongwe District, Ibex Hill, and Silverest, all in various districts of Zambia.

Our team conducted detailed assessments of the proposed construction sites to determine the potential environmental impacts and developed effective strategies to mitigate these impacts. We provided comprehensive reports outlining the findings of the EIAs and the measures that needed to be taken to ensure environmental sustainability.

Through our commitment to providing innovative and sustainable solutions, we were able to assist Harvest Group of Companies Limited in achieving their construction goals while ensuring that the environment and surrounding communities were protected. Our success in this project highlights our expertise in conducting EIAs and developing effective EPBs for various construction projects.

Conducting EIA and writing EPB’s for Block making factory for Deniz Limited Company

Eco-First Innovations conducted an environmental impact assessment and wrote environmental project briefs for a block making factory owned by Deniz Limited Company. The project was located in Makeni, Lusaka District and the assessment was conducted in November 2020. Additionally, another assessment was conducted in November 2021 for a similar factory located in Chibuluma, Kalulushi District. Through these assessments, Eco-First Innovations helped to ensure that the block making factories were operating in compliance with environmental regulations and that any potential negative impacts on the environment were minimized.

Conducting EIA and writing EPB’s for Filling station for JCB Limited Company

The project involved conducting an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and writing Environmental Project Briefs (EPBs) for a filling station owned by JCB Limited Company. The project was located on Mwinilunga Road and was carried out in February 2021. The aim of the EIA was to identify potential environmental impacts that may arise from the construction and operation of the filling station, and to propose measures to mitigate these impacts. The EPBs provided a detailed overview of the project and its potential environmental impacts, as well as proposed measures to ensure compliance with relevant environmental regulations and standards.

Facilitating Licence Acquisition for Oil spillages for Gatbro International Limited

The project involved assisting Gatbro International Limited in obtaining the necessary licenses required for oil spillages. The location of the project was on Mukwa Road, Lusaka District in October 2020. Eco-first Innovations provided support in navigating the regulatory requirements for obtaining the licenses and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. The project aimed to ensure that Gatbro International Limited could effectively manage any potential oil spillages that may occur in their operations.

Conducting EIA and writing EPB’s for Mega Church Construction for Winners Chapel International

The project involved conducting an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and writing Environmental Project Briefs (EPBs) for the construction of a mega church for Winners Chapel International in Libala, Lusaka District in March 2020. The purpose of the project was to assess the potential environmental impact of the church construction project and to identify mitigation measures to minimize any negative impact on the environment. Through this project, Eco-first Innovations worked with the church and relevant stakeholders to ensure that the construction project was environmentally sustainable and complied with all regulatory requirements.

Air quality and Noise measurements Tianyu Zambia Limited Company

The project involved conducting air quality and noise measurements for Tianyu Zambia Limited Company in Chilanga District. The aim of the project was to assess the level of air pollution and noise emissions from the company's operations and ensure compliance with the relevant regulations and standards. The measurements were carried out in July 2022, and the results were analyzed and presented in a comprehensive report. The report also included recommendations on measures that can be taken to minimize the company's environmental impact and improve air and noise quality in the surrounding areas.

In conclusion, Eco-First Innovations has had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects in different locations across Zambia. We have successfully conducted environmental impact assessments, written environmental project briefs, facilitated license acquisition, handled hazardous waste, conducted air/water/noise quality monitoring, and provided general environmental consultancy services. Our team has been dedicated to providing high-quality and timely services to our clients, ensuring that environmental and social safeguards are upheld. We are committed to continuing to provide innovative and sustainable solutions to meet the growing environmental challenges in Zambia and beyond.
